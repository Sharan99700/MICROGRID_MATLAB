🏛️ MPBSI — Five Sustainability Pillars

MPBSI integrates five mission-critical dimensions. Weights reflect military priority: TRI dominates (30%), EcSI second (25%) since long-run cost determines force sustainability. All weights are adjustable in the Optimise tab.

Default: TRI=0.30 · EcSI=0.25 · ORI=0.15 · LSI=0.15 · ESI=0.15

TRI — Tactical Resilience Index (30%)

TRI = 0.25×LPSP_score + 0.25×Autonomy_score + 0.25×Availability + 0.25×Storage_margin

LPSP Score: (1 − LPSP_critical) — zero tolerance policy enforced

Autonomy Score: min(1, effective_autonomy_days / 10) — target ≥ 10 days

Availability: (1 − LPSP_total) fraction of total hours served

Storage Margin: (Battery_kWh + H₂_kWh) / (Peak_kW × 7 × 24) — 7-day storage ratio

EcSI — Economic Sustainability Index (25%)

EcSI = max(0, min(1, 1 − LCOE / ₹42))

CAPEX: Σ(Component_size × Unit_cost)

Replacements (NPV): Battery @ yr 10, FC @ yr 10, EL @ yr 15

O&M: 2% CAPEX/yr annualized at 8% discount rate over 20 yr

LCOE: NPC / (Annual_load × 20 yr)

Benchmark: ₹42/kWh ≈ $0.50/kWh — military grid parity threshold

ORI — Operational Reliability Index (15%)

ORI = 0.30×Critical_surv + 0.30×Decentral + 0.20×Signature + 0.20×Blackstart

Critical Survivability: (1 − LPSP_critical) — Step 7 / Step 10 result

Decentralisation: PV(33%) + Wind(33%) + Battery(17%) + H₂(17%) score

Low Signature: 0.95 (zero diesel = zero IR/acoustic signature)

Black-start: 1.0 if both Battery + H₂ present, else 0.70

LSI — Logistics Support Index (15%)

LSI = 0.25×Fuel_elim + 0.20×Auto_support + 0.20×Maintenance + 0.15×Storage_life + 0.20×Supply

Fuel Elimination: 1.0 — no diesel supply chain (Akhnoor convoy risk eliminated)

Autonomy Support: min(1, effective_autonomy / 10)

Maintenance: 0.90 — renewable assets require minimal forward-base servicing

Storage Life: 0.85 — battery 10yr + H₂ tank 20yr combined durability

Supply Chain: 0.90 — locally sourced solar/wind, no fuel logistics

ESI — Energy Security Index (15%)

ESI = 0.30×Emission_avoid + 0.25×RES_pen + 0.25×Storage_util + 0.20×Lifecycle

Emission Avoidance: 1.0 — zero operational CO₂ (vs 2.68 kg/L diesel baseline)

RES Penetration: min(1, P_gen_annual / Load_annual)

Storage Utilisation: min(1, (Battery + H₂) / (RES_gen × 0.3))

Lifecycle Score: 0.85 — accounts for battery manufacturing footprint

Σ MPBSI Master Formula

MPBSI = 0.30×TRI + 0.25×EcSI + 0.15×ORI + 0.15×LSI + 0.15×ESI
